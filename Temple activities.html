Upcoming Activities

A grand celebration of 5th anniversary of temple will be performed as "Pancha Maha Vardhanthi" which includes a great, rare and auspicious "Shri Koti Namarchana - Tulasi Archana" by chanting the name of God a Crore times. This will commence from 20th April to 29th April , 2012. Followed by Chandika Hawan, Maha Annasantharpana (Mass Feeding), Religious and Cultural Programs. A visit of great number of piligrims are expected during this time.

Various types of Hindu rituals and poojas like Sathyanarayana Pooja, Grahashanthi Homa, Sandhishanthi Homa etc. also can be arranged at the temple. Utharakriyas, Shradda (ancestor worship pooja) and other aparakarmas can also be performed here as per requirements of deities along with food.

Knowing the needs of the Dakshina Kannada Hindu community, the temple has a beautifully decorated and spacious wedding hall which can be used for conducting marriages and other functions.
